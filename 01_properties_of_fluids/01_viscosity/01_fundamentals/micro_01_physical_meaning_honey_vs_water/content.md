
# The Great Descent

You drop a steel marble into **Water** and **Honey** at the exact same moment. 

## The Hook
Observe the differences in motion. One fluid yields easily, while the other seems to "grip" the marble.

## Probing Questions
1. Predict which ball reaches the bottom first.
2. Why specifically does the honey 'hold' the ball back? What is happening at the microscopic level?

## The "No-Slip" Mystery
Look closely at the particles directly touching the bottom plate in our simulation. They don't move at all. This is the **No-Slip Condition**.
